Rich Dad Poor Dad

Robert

This personal finance classic tells the story of Kiyosaki growing up with two influential fathers: his biological father (the "poor dad"), who was a highly educated government official but struggled financially, and his best friend's father (the "rich dad"), a wealthy entrepreneur. The book focuses on financial education, explaining the difference between assets (which put money in your pocket) and liabilities (which take money out). It challenges the idea that a high income is necessary for wealth and encourages building financial independence through investing, real estate, and starting businesses.

The God of Small Things

Arundhati Roy

This award-winning novel is a poignant story about fraternal twins, Esthappen and Rahel, whose childhood in Kerala, India, is fractured by the "Love Laws" of society. It explores how seemingly small, everyday events can have massive, life-altering consequences on people's lives and relationships.
